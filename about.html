About Briana

Briana Washington is a 19-year-old University student from Chicago. She is currently studying Telecommunictions. Briana has been published in the Young American Poetry Digest for 2010 and 2011 for two haikus. Another one of her short essays was also published online for the Chicago Teen Literary Festival as "Writer of the Week".
